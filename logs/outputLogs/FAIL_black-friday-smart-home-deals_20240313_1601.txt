Heading 2: < How Can You Be Sure You’re Getting the Best Price? > MATCHED

Block3_Paragraph 1 MATCHED

	Most traditional retailers offer some form of price matching. Show them an advertised price of the same product from a different retailer, and they’ll match that better price. Make sure to read the fine print, though; some stores don’t price match during the holiday shopping season.

Block3_Paragraph 2 MATCHED

	This year, some retailers are matching their own prices. If a fresh sale hits and the set of security cameras you bought is now advertised at a lower price, the store may refund you the difference. Again: Check the offer details for cutoff dates and ineligible items.

Block3_Paragraph 3 MATCHED

	Extended return windows are a staple of holiday shopping. Keep your receipts and note the return-by dates; items you bought as far back as October may still be returnable in January.
