Block2_Paragraph 1 MATCHED

	Exterior shutters cost between $50 and $1,000 (CAD 681 and CAD 1,361) per window, according to data from Fixr. Basic flat panel shutters start from around $50 (CAD 68) for a medium-sized pair. At the most expensive end of the scale, accordion-style shutters could set you back up to $1,000 (CAD 1,361) per window.

Block4_Paragraph 1 MATCHED

	Shutter installation costs can vary significantly, and understanding the factors affecting shutter prices can help you choose options to suit your budget. (Pricing data from Fixr.)

Block4_Sub Heading 2 MATCHED

	Size

Block4_Paragraph 3 MATCHED

	The larger your windows, the more you'll pay to install shutters. It costs around $40 to $200 (CAD 54 to CAD 272) to install decorative shutters on a small window measuring 16 inches by 20 inches. Meanwhile, installing shutters on an extra-large window could cost up to $1,200 (CAD 1,634) if you use premium materials.

Block4_Sub Heading 4 MATCHED

	Style

Block4_Paragraph 5 MATCHED

	The style of your new shutters significantly impacts the price. Flat panel shutters are the cheapest option at $50 to $300 (CAD 68 to CAD 408) per unit. Board and batten shutters are also relatively affordable at $50 to $600 (CAD 68 to CAD 817). Roller shutters are often the most expensive option.

Block4_Sub Heading 8 MATCHED

	Material

Block4_Paragraph 9 MATCHED

	Some shutter materials are more affordable than others. If you're on a tight budget, you can purchase affordable vinyl or faux wood shutters for around $100 to $300 (CAD 136 to CAD 408) per pair. Synthetic foam shutters, solid wood and aluminum options are midrange in terms of price. MDF is the most expensive shutter material at $300 to $700 (CAD 408 to CAD 953) per pair.

Block4_Sub Heading 10 MATCHED

	Opening Mechanism

Block4_Paragraph 11 MATCHED

	Most decorative shutters open manually. However, you can also purchase motorized shutters that open and shut when you push a button. Motorized units are more expensive, but they could be a good option if you want to increase your home's security or struggle to open shutters by hand. Manual shutters typically cost $300 to $700 (CAD 408 to CAD 953) per pair, while motorized models could cost almost twice as much.

Block5_Paragraph1_link 1 MATCHED

Block5_Paragraph 1 MATCHED

	Shutter installation is a common option for windows, but you can also use shutters to decorate exterior doors. As you might expect, shutter installation on a door is more expensive due to the size. Most homeowners spend around $100 to $800 (CAD 136 to CAD 1,089) per window, compared with $80 to $1,200 (CAD 109 to CAD 1,634) for a standard-sized door.

Heading 5: < How Much Does It Cost to Install Shutters on a Whole House? > MATCHED

Block6_Paragraph 1 MATCHED

	How much shutters cost for a whole house depends on several factors, including the size and number of windows and the shutter type. Most homeowners spend between $2,000 and $4,000 (CAD 2,723 and CAD 5,446) on whole-house shutter installation. Installing basic units on a small house could cost as little as $1,500 (CAD 2,042).
